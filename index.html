How to Play

Master the basics and become a legendary monster survivor

1

Move & Survive

Use WASD keys or touch controls to move your character. Avoid monster attacks while staying in the action to collect experience orbs.

2

Auto-Attack

Your character automatically attacks nearby enemies. Position yourself strategically to maximize damage while staying safe from harm.

3

Level Up

Collect experience orbs from defeated monsters to level up. Each level lets you choose from powerful upgrades and new abilities.

4

Build Strategy

Combine weapons, abilities, and passive upgrades to create devastating builds. Experiment with different combinations to find your perfect playstyle.
